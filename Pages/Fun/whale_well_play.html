Whale Well is a short adventure game where you play as a duck. Collect and trade items while exploring a civilization in the belly of a great whale.

Please download for a better experience including settings and saving.

Features:

• 18 items to collect and trade

• Over 20 characters

• Unfolding adventure game narrative

• A dozen locations to explore

Design, art, programming, and music by Ryan Nein.

Download available on Steam and itch.io.
