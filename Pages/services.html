Hire Me

Game Music Composition

I compose original music for games in a range of styles, from retro 8‑bit and 16‑bit soundtracks to modern electronic, ambient, and hybrid scores. My work is uniquely tailored, thematic, and built around the emotional tone of your project. I can create a single track or a full soundtrack. Pricing is negotiable and depends on scope, timeline, and complexity.

About My Work

I write music with a strong sense of rhythm, texture, and structure. I enjoy unusual time signatures, sharp grooves, and distinctive thematic ideas. I work entirely with digital instruments, synthesizers, and samples (no live recording) which allows for precise control over tone and style.

All music is composed and produced in Reaper, and I provide stems, loops, alt mixes, and implementation‑ready exports at no extra cost. I also handle mixing and mastering, and can adjust levels to match your game’s audio environment.

As a game developer myself, I approach every project with the same care and attention I give my own games.
